# Product Guide: Edge Framework

## Overview
Edge is a sophisticated, event-driven trading research and backtest repository. It provides a hardened, artifact-based pipeline for discovering, validating, and packaging trading strategies. The framework bridges the gap between raw market data research and high-fidelity strategy simulation.

## Target Users
*   **Quant Researchers:** Professionals focused on high-performance data processing and statistical signal discovery.
*   **Algo Traders:** Users building and simulating complex, production-ready trading strategies.
*   **System Architects:** Developers designing automated loops for autonomous strategy discovery and promotion.

## Core Goals
*   **Reliability:** Ensure artifact reproducibility, auditability, and pipeline stability.
*   **Discovery Yield:** Maximize the efficiency and breadth of event-driven candidate discovery.
*   **Automation:** Streamline the transition from research-grade signals to deployable Nautilus strategies.

## Key Features & Priorities
*   **High-Fidelity Event Detectors:** Maintain and expand a robust inventory of market event detectors (e.g., Climax Volume, Liquidity Stress).
*   **Synthetic Research Environment:** Advanced synthetic data generation with truth-map validation for hardening detectors.
*   **Performance Optimization:** Identifying and resolving performance bottlenecks in the research pipeline to handle larger datasets.
*   **Nautilus Integration:** Seamless integration with the Nautilus runtime for strategy certification and backtesting.

## Future Vision
*   **Live Trading Integration:** Evolving from a research and backtesting framework into a real-time execution engine.
*   **Autonomous AI Research Loop:** Leveraging AI and RL to explore the strategy search space and optimize detector parameters.
